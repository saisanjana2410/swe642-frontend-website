Computer Science Department

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University is one of the largest and most comprehensive computer science programs in the region. It offers innovative programs in computer science and information technology, focusing on cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, software engineering, data science, and more. The department emphasizes both theory and practical skills, preparing students to excel in academic research, industry, and beyond.

With world-class faculty and strong ties to government and industry, GMU’s CS Department provides unique opportunities for students to engage in groundbreaking research and real-world problem solving.
